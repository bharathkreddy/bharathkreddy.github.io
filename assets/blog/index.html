Hypothesis testing.

Python Data Analytics Mar 05, 2020

One of the most ignored aspect of forming an opinion based on a subset of possible observations is hypothesis testing. For organizations, areas where hypothesis testing should never be ignored are Audit, Control testings, gauge effectiveness of training or any other management interventions. Some areas where it should be explored would be to understand effects of multiple factors & interactions of factors on any desired variable like - attrition, Errors, data breaches etc....

The process of induction is the process of assuming the simplest law that can be made to harmonize with our experience. This process, however, has no logical foundation but only a psychological one. It is clear that there are no grounds for believing that the simplest course of events will really happen.

Ludwig Wittgenstein
